## Design & UX Considerations

### Color Scheme
- Primary: Deep Forest Green (#2D5016 or similar)
- Secondary: Light cream/off-white (#F5F5F0)
- Accent: Warm wood tones
- Text: Dark charcoal on light backgrounds

### Typography
- Headings: Bold, industrial serif or modern sans-serif
- Body: Clean, readable sans-serif
- Emphasis: Green accents for CTAs and key points

### Image Strategy
- Use original Squarespace photography where available
- High-quality product/project photography
- Before/after imagery for projects
- Team and facility photography
- Sustainability/nature imagery

### Interactive Elements
- Service cards with hover effects
- Image carousels for furniture and projects
- Expandable FAQ sections
- Contact form with validation
- Google Map integration for location
